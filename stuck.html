Safety Tips

1. Turn on your hazard lights.
2. Move your vehicle to a safe location.
3. Stay inside your vehicle if it's unsafe outside.
4. Call for help if needed.
5. Keep your seatbelt fastened until help arrives.
6. Be aware of your surroundings.
